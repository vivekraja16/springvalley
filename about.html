About Spring Valley

SV is developed and promoted by B&B Developers and Builders. It is located at Malaikodi, a southern suburb of Vellore town. Spring Valley has 55 villas spread over 4 acres of land. It has single bedroom and two bedroom villas designed to be friendly for the elderly.
