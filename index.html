Spring Valley Retirement Haven

Spring Valley retirement home is a haven for senior citizens, a place for the elderly to enjoy their sunset years in a friendly, safe and comfortable environment.

Mission

Spring Valley is committed to providing holistic care encompassing the physical and emotional needs of the elderly. It will strive to be creative and innovative to enrich quality of life.

Vision

Endorse creative, innovative, dynamic approaches to care for the elderly Promote wellness and healthy lifestyles Encourage warm and friendly relationships Support mutual respect and understanding Create an atmosphere that emphasises positive attitudes Maintain a secure and safe environment.

An ongoing endeavour

Spring Valley is an on-going endeavour to grow to be a leader in providing high quality care for the elderly. We envisage growth in all aspects of care and support for the elderly, learning from our own experiences and by keeping ourselves updated on advances in order to meet the evolving needs of the community.
